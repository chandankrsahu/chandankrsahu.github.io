Home Automation using Google Assistant

The idea behind Google assistant-controlled Home automation is to control home devices with voice.In this home automation, as the user gives commands to the Google assistant, Home appliances like Bulb, Fan and Motor etc., can be controlled accordingly.

The microcontroller used is NodeMCU (ESP8266) and the communication between the microcontroller and the applica- tion is established via Wi-Fi (Internet).The Tech Stack Mainly consisted of Adafruit,MQTT,Arduino.io,IFTTT,etc;
